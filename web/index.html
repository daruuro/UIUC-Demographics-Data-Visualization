Project #1: Gender and Racial Diversity at UIUC, 2005-2015

by Abdullahi, Angelos, Peng, and Yuxin

Introduction

A simple visualization of demographics changes at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, over the decade 2005 to 2015. The dataset for this visualization was provided by the Division of Management Information at UIUC, was cleaned and processed using Pandas, and visualized using D3.js.
